What is NurTravel?
NurTravel is a travel agency that offers customized tours, adventure packages, and holiday destinations worldwide. We aim to provide an unforgettable travel experience with expert guidance and personalized services.
How can I book a tour with NurTravel?
You can book a tour with us by visiting our website, browsing the destinations, and filling out the booking form. You can also reach out to our customer service team to help plan your trip and finalize the booking process.
What should I do if I need to cancel or reschedule my booking?
If you need to cancel or reschedule your booking, please contact our customer service team at least 48 hours before your tour departure. We will assist you with the necessary changes according to our cancellation policy.
Is there a refund policy for canceled tours?
Yes, NurTravel has a refund policy for canceled tours. The amount refunded will depend on the cancellation period and the terms of the booking. Please check the cancellation policy on our website or contact us for more details.
